What we do Our Services

Lawn Mowing

Fresh Lawn has thousands of Happy Residential & Commercial clients that enjoy a freshly mowed lawn every week. Our Quality is the foundation of our success!

Bush Trimming

Professional Shrub & Bush trimming will keep your lawn looking beautiful and maintained. We offer scheduled or on demand bush trimming services.

Flower Bed Weeding

Fresh Lawn offers Flower Bed Weeding services. You can choose the frequency of services, One Time, Weekly, Bi-Weekly or you can set up a custom service.

Fertilization

Your Lawn uses a great deal of energy to stay healthy during the groing season, so Fertilization helps keep your lawn healthy and promotes new leaf and root growth.

Mulch Installation

Mulch helps retain soil moister and improves the look of your landscaping. Mulch is also an excellent way to add nutrients back into your soil as it breaks down.

Leaf Pick Up

Lets face it, it's going to happen every year, trees shed thier foliage and it can make your landscape look terrible. Fresh Lawn offers leaf pick up and bagging services.

Rye Grass Overseeding

Great for making the grass areas that are dormant green again during the cooler times of the year. Not very expensive and looks great.

Gutter Cleaning

Great for making the grass areas that are dormant green again during the cooler times of the year. Not very expensive and looks great.

Lot Mowing

We provide lot mowing on 1.5 acres or more.

Snow Removal

We provide Snow Removal services to remove snow from your driveway and sidewalks.

Artificial Turf

We sell Real Turf! Looking for a DIY, we will show you how to install and we will ship you the turf.

Flower Planting

We offer Flower & Shrub planting.
